Time Woven into Gold

Time Woven into Gold is a 5-star Light Cone for the path of Remembrance. It is Aglaea’s Signature and Best-in-Slot (BiS) Lightcone.

"This strand, a soldier's sacrifice."
"This strand, a hero's growth."

The Garmentmaker pulls at the threads using a needle, everything held between her fingers.
"The destiny of certain heroes has already been severed..."

"The fates of certain heroes must continue..."
The divine-bestowed loom continues to work, and the Garmentmakers dance with lively grace. Eons of time had already passed through the loom, and she has long been accustomed to such waiting....

For the sake of turning time to golden "Inspiration."
