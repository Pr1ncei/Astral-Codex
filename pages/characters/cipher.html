Eidolons

E1: Read the Room, Seek the Glee

Cipher's tally of damage is 150% of its original value. When using the Talent's Follow-up attack, increases Cipher's attack by 80%, lasting for 2 turn(s).


E2: In the Fray, Nab On a Spree

When Cipher hits an enemy target, there is a 120% base chance to increase the damage it receives by 30%, lasting for 2 turn(s).


E3: From Thin Air, Hard to Foresee

Increases the level of Cipher's Ultimate by two with a maximum upgrade level of 15, and increases the level of her basic attack by one, with a maximum upgrade level of 10.


E4: The Jig Is Up, Quick to Flee

After the 'Patron' gets attacked by an ally target, Cipher deals Quantum Additional damage to it equal to 50% of Cipher's attack.


E5: Safe in Numbers, Light as a Bee

Increases the level of Cipher's Skill and Talent by two with a maximum upgrade Level of 15.


E6: The Thief's Game, Unsung and Free

The damage dealt by Follow-up attack from Cipher's Talent increases by 350%. During the tally, an additional 16% of non-Overflow damage dealt by this attack is tallied. After the tally is cleared by using Ultimate, 20% of the cleared tally is returned.

Best Relics and Ornaments

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters

As a CRIT DPS that can implant debuffs, Cipher's best-in-slot relic is the Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters since it provides CRIT stats and deals more damage to debuffed enemies.

Eagle of Twilight Line

Eagle of Twilight Line is the best relic if Cipher focuses more on debuffing. With an Energy Regen rope, Cipher can access her Ult sooner, and her high SPD leads to more debuff application.

Genius of Brilliant Stars

Genius of Brilliant Stars can be used if the enemy has a Quantum Weakness. This can dish out more damage than Pioneer if the enemy has Quantum Weakness and has their DEF lowered.

Firmament Frontline Glamoth

As Cipher is ideally built with 170 SPD, her best ornament is the Firmament: Frontline Glamoth since it provides the full DMG boost at the 160 SPD threshold.

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas

If Cipher is used more as a debuffer, Lushaka will be her best option since it has Energy Regen and can buff the ATK of your Main DPS.

Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm

Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm can be considered for more CRIT Rate if Cipher is used in Acheron teams.

Best Lightcones

Lies Dance on the Breeze

Cipher's best Light Cone since it provides her SPD stats and DEF shred that can be repeatedly inflicted.

Resolution Shines As Pearls of Sweat

Cipher's best 4-star Light Cone for both Sub-DPS and Debuffer builds. While this Light Cone doesn't help much with Cipher's personal damage, it greatly benefits the Main DPS.

Holiday Thermae Escapade

Increases the DMG dealt by Cipher and allows her to apply Vulnerability debuffs.

Boundless Choreo

Provides CRIT Rate and deals more CRIT DMG to Slowed down or DEF reduced enemies. This requires allies that can provide Slow and DEF reduction to work.
