Best Relics and Ornaments

Wavestrider Captain

Wavestrider Captain is Phainon's best Relic set since it provides him Help stacks which he can consume by using his Ultimate for an ATK buff.

The ATK buff will last until Phainon exits his Enhanced State.

Scholar Lost in Erudition

Scholar Lost in Erudition provides Phainon a CRIT Rate buff and increases his Skill and Ultimate DMG.

Champion of Streetwise Boxing

Champion of Streetwise Boxing is a great early-game set since it increases Phainon's ATK whenever he gets hit.

Arcadia of Woven Dreams

Arcadia of Woven Dreams is Phainon's best Ornament since it provides him a DMG buff when there are fewer party members on the field.

Phainon's Enhanced State will temporarily remove other allies from combat, so this enables the full DMG buff.

Rutilant Arena

Rutilant Arena is great on Phainon since it increases his CRIT Rate, Basic ATK DMG, and Skill DMG. The buff will carry over to his Enhanced

Best Lightcones

Thus Burns the Dawn

Thus Burns the Dawn is Phainon's best Light Cone since it helps ignore DEF and increase his DMG after he uses his Ultimate. The Base SPD boost is a nice bonus for Phainon's Ultimate.

On the Fall of an Aeon

On the Fall of an Aeon is Phainon's second-best option since it provides an ATK buff and increases his DMG when he inflicts Weakness Break on enemies.

A Trail of Bygone Blood

A Trail of Bygone Blood is Phainon's best 4-star Light Cone since it provides CRIT Rate and a boost to his Skill and Ultimate DMG.

Something Irreplaceable

Something Irreplaceable is an option that can work with Phainon since it increases his DMG when he attacks or gets hit.
