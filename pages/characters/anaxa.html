Best Relics and Ornaments

Genius of Brilliant Stars

Anaxa's best relic set especially with the Life Should Be Cast to Flames equipped. This is because Anaxa can implant a Quantum weakness onto enemies and activate the set's DEF ignore.

Scholar Lost in Erudition

Scholar Lost in Erudition is an all-around great Relic set for Anaxa since it increases the damage of his Skill which he often uses.

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters

Pioneer Diver of Dead Waters is a good alternative since it helps deal increased damage to debuffed enemies. Since Anaxa can inflict debuffs, he can fully utilize this relic set.

Rutilant Arena

Rutilant Arena is Anaxa's best ornament since it provides CRIT Rate and increased Skill DMG. This can be used for any team Anaxa is on.

Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm

Izumo Gensei and Takama Divine Realm is a great alternative only in Dual DPS teams since it increases Anaxa's CRIT Rate as long as there's an Erudition unit in the party.

Best Lightcones

Life Should Be Cast to Flames

Anaxa's signature Light Cone that provides a bit of energy restoration and a huge damage buff. This also ignores enemy DEF when Anaxa attacks.

Into the Unreachable Veil

Increases Anaxa's CRIT Rate. This increases Anaxa's Skill DMG and regenerates a Skill Point after he uses his Ultimate

The Great Cosmic Enterprise

Anaxa's best 4-star Light Cone. It increases Anaxa's attack deals more damage depending on the amount of type weaknesses an enemy has.

Today is Another Peaceful Day

Provides a permanent damage boost to Anaxa. This Light Cone also has a high base ATK.

The Day The Cosmos Fell

The Day The Cosmos Fell is Anaxa's free-to-play Light Cone that can increase his CRIT DMG if he attacks and two enemies have a corresponding Weakness to Wind. Since Anaxa can implant missing Weaknesses, he will have no problem activating the passive.
