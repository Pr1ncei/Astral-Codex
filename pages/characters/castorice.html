Best Relics and Ornaments

Poet of Mourning Collapse

Castorice's best-in-slot relic set since this will increase her Quantum DMG and the CRIT Rate of both her and her Memosprite.

Longevous Disciple

Another great Relic for Castorice since this provides an HP increase to Castorice and can increase her CRIT Rate whenever she consumes her HP.

Genius of Brilliant Stars

Genius of Brilliant Stars can increase Castorice's Quantum DMG and ignore enemy DEF.

Do note that the 4pc. effect will not apply to Castorice's Memosprite.

Bone Collection’s Serene Demesne

Castorice's best ornament as it increases her and her memosprite's CRIT DMG. The effect can be easily activated since Castorice has a lot of HP.

Arcadia of Woven Dreams

Used by Castorice for the DMG buff it provides if there are more than 4 allies in combat (Memosprites included). This works if there are two or more other Remembrance characters in the party.

The Wondrous BananAmusement Park

The third-best option since it can increase Castorice's CRIT DMG as long as her Memosprite is on the field.

Best Lightcones

Make Farewells More Beautiful

Castorice's best-in-slot Light Cone that provides a huge boost in HP and can ignore enemy DEF. This will also advance Castorice in the turn order when her memosprite disappears.

The Flower Remembers

Castorice's best 4-star Light Cone since this provides CRIT DMG and her Memosprite deals increased CRIT DMG.

Sweat Now, Cry Less

Another great 4-star Light Cone since this provides a CRIT Rate boost and increased DMG for when her Memosprite deals DMG.

Flame Afar

A F2P option for players that can be used for temporary solution because its ATK-scaling effect is not beneficial for Castorice’s HP-scaling kit.
