Eidolons

E1: Drift at the Whim of Venus

The enemy afflicted with "Seam Stitch" takes 15% increased DMG. After Aglaea or Garmentmaker attacks this target, additionally regenerates 20 Energy.


E2: Sail on the Raft of Eyelids

When Aglaea or Garmentmaker takes action, enables the DMG dealt by Aglaea and Garmentmaker to ignore 14% of the target's DEF. This effect stacks up to 3 time(s) and lasts until any unit, other than Aglaea or Garmentmaker, actively uses an ability.


E3: Bequeath in the Coalescence of Dew

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10. Memosprite Talent Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.


E4: Flicker Below the Surface of Marble

The SPD Boost effect from the Memosprite Talent has its max stack limit increased by 1. After Aglaea uses an attack, can also make Garmentmaker gain the SPD Boost effect from the Memosprite Talent.


E5: Weave Under the Shroud of Woe

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15. Memosprite Skill Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.


E6: Fluctuate in the Tapestry of Fates

While Aglaea is in "Supreme Stance," increases her and Garmentmaker's Lightning RES PEN by 20%. When Aglaea's or Garmentmaker's SPD is greater than 160/240/320, the Joint ATK DMG dealt by them increases by 10%/30%/60%.

Best Relics and Ornaments

Hero of Triumphant Song

Aglaea's best-in-slot relic set since this will increase her SPD and CRIT DMG. Garmentmaker attacks frequently so Aglaea can have the full uptime of the Hero of Triumphant Song.

Musketeer of Wild Wheat

Musketeer of Wild Wheat is a great budget option that provides bonus SPD and Basic ATK DMG for Aglaea.

The Wondrous BananAmusement Park

Aglaea's best ornament since it provides a whopping 48% CRIT DMG for having a summon.

Rutilant-Arena

The second-best option since it can increase Aglaea's Basic ATK DMG as long as she has at least 70% CRIT Rate.

Firmament Frontline Glamoth

Firmament Frontline Glamoth can also be used by Aglaea since it provides a DMG boost if Aglaea hits the 135 or 160 SPD thresholds.

Best Lightcones

Time Woven Into Gold

The best Light Cone for Aglaea since it provides a base SPD Bonus. This will also increase Aglaea's CRIT DMG every time Garmentmaker attacks and a Basic ATK DMG boost after attaining max stacks.

Sweat Now, Cry Less

Sweat Now, Cry Less provides a CRIT Rate boost and increased DMG for when Garmentmaker deals DMG.

Genuises' Greetings

Increases Aglaea's ATK and provides a Basic ATK DMG boost after she uses an Ultimate. This loses a lot of value if Aglaea receives a lot of CRIT DMG buffs from party buffs.

Reminiscense

Provides a normal DMG boost when Garmentmaker's turn starts.
