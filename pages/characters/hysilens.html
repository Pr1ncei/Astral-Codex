E1: You Ask Why Hearts Cry

While Hysilens is on the field, the DoT dealt by ally targets is equal to 116% of their original value. E6: When to Return From Where You Lie

While the Zone is active, at the start of each turn or after one attack by an ally target, increases the maximum trigger count for Hysilens's Physical DoT effect to 12, and increases the multiplier of the DMG dealt by 20%.

Best Relics and Ornaments

Prisoner in Deep Confinement

Prisoner in Deep Confinement is the best Relic set for DoT units like Hysilens. This can help her Hysilens enemy DEF with her DoTs.

Champion of Streetwise Boxing

Champion of Streetwise Boxing is a great early-game set since it increases Hysilens' Physical DMG and ATK stats.

Revelry by the Sea

Revelry by the Sea is Hysilens' best Ornament thanks to DoT DMG buff it provides when she has 3600 ATK.

Firmament Frontline Glamoth

Firmament Frontline Glamoth is a solid alternative since this increases Hysilens' DMG if she has at least 135 SPD.

Best Lightcones

Why Does the Ocean Sing

Why Does the Ocean Sing is Hysilens' best-in-slot Light Cone since it provides Effect Hit Rate and a DoT buff for every debuff inflicted by her. This also provides a SPD increase to allies when they attack an enemy.

Reforged Rememberance

Reforged Remembrance is great on Hysilens since it provides a Effect Hit Rate buff, and she can use it to get Prophet stacks that increase her ATK and ignore enemy DEF.

Eyes of the Prey

Eyes of the Prey is Hysilens' best 4-star Light Cone since it increases her Effect Hit Rate and DoT.

Good Night and Sleep Well

Good Night and Sleep Well increases Hysilens' DMG dealt for every DoT or debuff applied to the enemy target

It's Showtime

It’s Showtime is Hysilens' free-to-play option that increases her DMG when enemies are debuffed and increases her ATK based on her Effect Hit Rate.
