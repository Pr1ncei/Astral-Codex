Eidolons

E1: Frost Hones Spine of Steel

The damage multiplier applied by 'Godslayer Be God' to the primary target increases by 30%, and Godslayer Be God becomes Imaginary Damage dealt to all enemies equal to the damage multiplier applied to the primary target.


E2: Strife Beholds Cry of Dead

During 'Vendetta', the damage dealt by Mydei ignores 15% of enemy targets' defense, and when receiving healing, Mydei converts 40% of the healed amount to Charge. The tally of the converted Charge cannot exceed 40 points, and resets this tally of Charge after any unit takes action.


E3: Honor Exalts Feast of Faith

Increases the level of Mydei's Skill by two with a maximum upgrade level of 15, and increases the level of his Basic Attack by one, with a maximum upgrade level of 10.


E4: Siren Jolts the Laconic Lion

While in his Vendetta state, Mydei increases his Crit Damage by 30% and restores HP by 10% of his Max HP, after receiving attacks from enemy targets.


E5: War Chisels Flesh of Flame

Increases the level of Mydei's Ultimate and Talent by two, with a maximum upgrade Level of 15.


E6: Legacy Scales Mound of Blood

When entering battle, Mydei immediately enters the Vendetta state, and lowers the Charge required for Godslayer Be God to 100 points.

Best Relics and Ornaments

Scholar Lost in Erudition

Scholar Lost in Erudition is Mydei's best Relic set since most of Mydei's DMG will come from his Skill. This will also increase his CRIT Rate by 8%

Longevous Disciple

Longevous Disciple is a great Relic for Mydei since he can consume his own HP to get up to 16% CRIT Rate.

Bone Collection’s Serene Demesne

Bone Collection's Serene Demesne is Mydei's best ornament since he can easily reach the 5000 HP threshold for the CRIT DMG bonus.

Rutilant Arena

Rutilant Arena is the second-best option since it can increase Mydei's Skill DMG as long as he has at least 70% CRIT Rate.

Best Lightcones

Flame of Blood, Blaze My Path

Flame of Blood, Blaze My Path is Mydei's signature Light Cone. It increases his Max HP and incoming healing. It also gives him a DMG boost when using his Skill and Ultimate.

The Unreachable Side

The Unreachable Side is a strong Light Cone on Mydei since it provides a boost to CRIT Rate and HP%, and increases his DMG when he consumes his HP.

Ninja Record - Sound Hunt

Ninja Record - Sound Hunt is Mydei's best 4-star Light Cone since it provides HP% and increases his CRIT DMG whenever he consumes his HP or restores it.

Flame Afar

Flames Afar is a more accessible Light Cone that increases Mydei's DMG if he consumes enough HP. This also provides some more self-healing.
