Eidolons

E1: Seize the Crowns of All

The character with "Military Merit" ignores 16% of the targets' DEF when dealing DMG. If "Military Merit" has been upgraded to "Peerage," then the character additionally ignores 20% of the targets' DEF when dealing Skill DMG. When Cerydra uses her Skill, regenerates 2 Energy for the designated ally target.


E2: Forge the Dreams of Many

The character with "Military Merit" deals 40% increased DMG. While a teammate on the field has "Military Merit," Cerydra's DMG dealt increases by 160%.


E3: Torch the Laws of Old

Skill Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
Basic ATK Lv. +1, up to a maximum of Lv. 10.


E4: Remake the Realms of Men

Increases Ultimate's DMG multiplier by 240%.


E5: Help and Hurt Repaid in Full

Ultimate Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.
Talent Lv. +2, up to a maximum of Lv. 15.


E6: A Journey Set Starward

The character with "Military Merit" increases their All-Type RES PEN by 20%, and the multiplier for the Additional DMG triggered via "Military Merit" increases by 300%. While a teammate on the field has "Military Merit," Cerydra's All-Type RES PEN increases by 20%.

Best Relics and Ornaments

Sacerdos' Relived Ordeal

Cerydra's best Relic set since it provides a CRIT DMG buff to the DPS. Cerydra can easily use this since her Skill and Ultimate can only target one ally.

Eagle of Twilight Line

Cerydra can also use the Eagle of Twilight Line since it gives her Advance Forward after using her Ultimate, which means she gets her next turn much earlier.

This will get Cerydra's Ultimate back up more easily by restoring Energy through quick turns.

Lushaka, the Sunken Seas

Cerydra's best ornament for general use since this provides her Energy Regen and buffs the DPS unit's ATK.

Sprightly Vonwacq

Sprightly Vonwacq is also great for Cerydra since it allows her to have her turn advanced at the beginning of combat.

This is best used in tandem with the Eagle of Twilight Line Relic set.

Penacony, Land of the Dreams

Penacony, Land of the Dreams is an excellent choice if Cerydra is paired up with Wind DPS units like Anaxa or Feixiao.

Best Lightcones

Epoch Etched in Golden Blood

Cerydra's best Light Cone since it provides a high ATK buff and increases the Skill DMG of the DPS.

Flowing Nightglow

Provides Cerydra some Energy Regen and increases her ATK after she uses her Ultimate. When Cerydra uses her Ultimate, she can provide a DMG buff to the entire party.

A Grounded Ascent

A Grounded Ascent is another strong alternative for its Energy regeneration and the DMG buff it provides.

The Forever Victual

Cerydra's best 4-star Light Cone since it provides an ATK buff that can be increased every time she uses her Skill.

Planetary Rendezvous

Planetary Rendezvous can be great with Cerydra if she's paired up with a Wind DPS.

Dance! Dance! Dance!

Dance! Dance! Dance! provides turn advance to the entire party whenever Cerydra uses her Ultimate.
