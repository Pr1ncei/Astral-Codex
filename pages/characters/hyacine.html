E1: Cradle the Candle of Night

When Hyacine is in the "After Rain" state, additionally increases the Max HP of all ally targets by 50%, and immediately restores HP equal to 8% of Hyacine's Max HP after using an attack.

When any ally target's HP decreases, this unit's SPD increases by 30% for 2 turn(s).

E6: O Sky, Heed My Plea

Little Ica uses Memosprite Skill and changes the amount of Healing tally cleared to 12%. When Little Ica is on the field, increases all ally targets' All-Type RES PEN by 20%.

HYACINE'S BEST BUILD
AND TEAM

Best Relics and Ornaments

Warrior Goddess of Sun and Thunder

Warrior Goddess of Sun and Thunder is Hyacine's best-in-slot relic set since this provides SPD and increases the party's CRIT DMG through healing.

Giant Tree of Rapt Brooding

Hyacine's best ornament since it increases her SPD and provides more healing depending on her SPD stat.

Broken Keel

Broken Keel can be used to increase the party's CRIT DMG. You don't need to build Hyacine with Effect RES since she has enough of that through her Bonus Ability.

Best Lightcones

Long May Rainbows Adorn the Sky

Hyacine's signature Light Cone that provides useful buffs for the party. This provides a huge SPD increase for Hyacine and increases the damage taken by enemies.

Memory's Curtain Never Falls

More accessible Light Cone that provides SPD and increases the party's DMG when Hyacine uses her Skill.

Victory In a Blink

Another accessible Light Cone that increases Hyacine's CRIT DMG and buffs the party's DMG when Hyacine uses her Skill. The CRIT DMG buff is usable since Hyacine can always Crit.
